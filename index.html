Our Story

Every successful journey begins with direction. That's where our name comes from.

NAVI — Navigation

Finding the right path before everyone else. Discovering opportunities hidden inside massive amounts of information. Seeing where markets are heading before they become obvious. For us, navigation is more than movement — it's intelligence, applied to millions of ecommerce products, consumer trends, supply chains, and shifting customer behavior.

AGI — the philosophy, not the claim

AGI traditionally stands for Artificial General Intelligence. True AGI remains a future ambition for the industry — but we embrace its underlying philosophy: systems that continuously learn, analyze, adapt, and improve business decisions using today's most advanced AI, not a single model working alone.

We believe AI is not replacing entrepreneurs — it is empowering them. NAVI AGI exists to bring human creativity and artificial intelligence together to build the next generation of ecommerce companies.

Growth Roadmap

A staged path from marketplace operator to global infrastructure.

01

Marketplace Foundations

Establish strong operations across leading online marketplaces — Amazon, Walmart Marketplace, eBay, and Etsy. Use AI-driven research to identify high-potential product categories, optimize listings, and improve inventory planning. Expand warehouse and fulfillment capabilities across the United States.

02

Independent Brands

Transition from marketplace-first operations to a portfolio of independent ecommerce brands with dedicated online stores — a recognizable house of brands supported by centralized technology, logistics, and AI-powered decision systems.

03

Global Infrastructure

Scale internationally, beginning with North America. Build a world-class ecommerce infrastructure capable of processing more than 30,000 customer orders every month while maintaining exceptional operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
